2. Description of Service

MacPulse is a remote control application that allows you to:

  • Lock your Mac's screen remotely
  • Put your Mac to sleep remotely
  • Shutdown your Mac remotely
  • Pair up to 3 Mac devices securely through iCloud

The App consists of:

  • MacPulse iOS app (for iPhone)
  • MacPulseHelper Mac app (for macOS)

Both apps are required for the service to function.
