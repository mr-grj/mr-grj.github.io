Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

Introduction

MacPulse ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MacPulse iOS app and MacPulseHelper Mac app (collectively, the "Services").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Device Names: Your iPhone and Mac device names (e.g., "Alex's iPhone," "Alex's MacBook Pro")
  • Pairing Codes: Temporary 6-digit codes generated during device pairing (expired after 5 minutes)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifiers: Unique identifiers generated to securely pair your devices
  • Command Data: Records of commands sent (lock, sleep, shutdown) including timestamps
  • iCloud Account: Your iCloud account is used to sync data between your devices

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not track your browsing history or app usage
  • We do not collect any personal information beyond what is necessary for the app to function
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate and pair your iPhone with your Mac
  • Securely transmit commands from your iPhone to your Mac
  • Verify the authenticity of commands using cryptographic signatures
  • Display device names in the app interface

Data Storage and Security

CloudKit Private Database

All data is stored in your private CloudKit database, which means:

  • Only you can access this data
  • Apple encrypts data in transit and at rest
  • We (the app developers) cannot access your data
  • Data is tied to your iCloud account

Data Retention

  • Pairing Codes: Deleted automatically after 5 minutes
  • Commands: Deleted automatically after execution (typically within seconds)
  • Pairing Information: Stored until you manually unpair devices
  • Revocation Records: Deleted after 24 hours

Security Measures

  • All commands are signed with HMAC-SHA256 cryptographic signatures
  • Shared secrets are stored securely in the device Keychain
  • Commands older than 5 minutes are automatically rejected
  • Rate limiting prevents abuse (maximum 10 commands per minute)

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Apple iCloud

MacPulse uses Apple's CloudKit service to sync data between your devices. Apple's privacy policy applies to this data: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

No Third-Party Sharing

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services
  • We do not transfer data outside of Apple's CloudKit infrastructure

Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

You can view your CloudKit data through:

  • The MacPulse Settings screen (paired devices count)
  • Apple's CloudKit Dashboard (requires developer access)

Children's Privacy

MacPulse is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Continued use of MacPulse after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

MacPulse stores data in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure, which may be located in various countries. By using MacPulse, you consent to the transfer of data to these locations.
